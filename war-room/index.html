30-Day Launch Playbook
Week-by-week battle plan to go from zero to first 100 orders across all three platforms.
Week 1 — Foundation
Store Setup & Initial Listings
Register stores on all 3 platforms. Upload first 10 products from Product Radar's top picks. Focus on the top 4 seasonal winners for April (cooling products, portable fans, skincare recovery, waterproof accessories).
  • Register Shopee TH store (3-month free commission window)
  • Register Lazada TH store (higher margins, premium positioning)
  • Register TikTok Shop TH (viral content channel)
  • Set up CJ Dropshipping account — connect to all 3 platforms
  • Use CJ Thailand warehouse for faster local delivery
  • Create Thai-language listings with SEO keywords (ชื่อสินค้า + คำค้นหา)
  • Upload 5+ product photos per SKU (lifestyle + white background + detail shots)
Week 2 — Content & Social Proof
Video Content Blitz
Film 15-second TikTok-style product demos. "Before/after" format works best for beauty. "Unboxing reaction" for gadgets. "Happy pet" for pet products. Goal: 3 videos per product minimum.
  • Create 10-15 short-form videos (15-30 sec) for TikTok + Shopee Video
  • Hire 2-3 Thai micro-influencers (฿500-2,000/post) via TikTok Creator Marketplace
  • Set up Shopee Affiliate program — let creators earn commission
  • Seed initial reviews: offer first 20 buyers a ฿50 discount for honest review
  • Enable Shopee Live — do first livestream selling (evening 8-10 PM optimal)
  • Create LINE Official Account for post-purchase support
Week 3 — Paid Acceleration
Ads & Campaign Optimization
Start Shopee Ads with ฿200/day budget on top 5 SKUs. Monitor CTR and conversion for 3 days, then double down on winners. Kill anything below 2% conversion rate.
  • Shopee Ads: Search ads (฿200/day) — target category keywords
  • Shopee Ads: Discovery ads (฿100/day) — algorithmic placement
  • TikTok Ads: Spark Ads on best-performing organic videos (฿150/day)
  • Lazada: Join upcoming Mega Campaign (register 2 weeks early)
  • Set up "Free Shipping" badge on Shopee (massive conversion booster)
  • A/B test product titles and main images — Shopee rewards higher CTR
  • Monitor competitor pricing daily — use Margin Calculator to verify viability
Week 4 — Scale & Systematize
Double Down on Winners
Analyze first 30 days. Identify top 3 SKUs by profit (not just revenue). Expand those SKU families. Cut losers ruthlessly. Begin building repeat customer base.
  • Analyze: Which 3 products generated most profit? Expand to color/size variants
  • Kill: Remove any product below 30% margin after fees
  • Bundle: Create combo deals (e.g., ice roller + face mask = ฿599 set)
  • Restock: Pre-stock top winners at CJ Thailand warehouse for 3-day delivery
  • Automate: Set up order fulfillment automation CJ → platforms
  • Plan: Research May products (Mother's Day, summer continuation)
  • Target: 100 total orders across all platforms by Day 30
