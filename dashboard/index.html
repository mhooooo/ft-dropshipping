  • Shipping (25.9% of costs) — Biggest lever. Negotiate CJ volume shipping at 50+ orders/day. Target ฿80-90/order to save ~฿5K/month
  • Packaging (9.1%) — Buy branded poly mailers in bulk (฿15-20/unit at 1000+). Current ฿35 avg is high
  • Platform fees (5.7%) — Join Lazada's preferred seller program for reduced commission (2.5% vs 4%)
  • Ad spend (17%) — Focus on TikTok organic content to reduce paid dependency. Target 30% organic traffic by Month 6
  • Potential savings: Optimizing top 3 cost levers could improve net margin from 16.7% → 22-24%
